Friday · June 19

Arrival + Coki food stand + The Greenhouse

First-day local beach food
12:00 PM
Land in St. Thomas. Pick up bags and rental car.
1:00 PM-ish
Early Airbnb check-in. Drop bags, change, and get beach-ready without rushing.
1:30–3:45 PM
Coki Beach. Go for the food stand, quick swim/snorkel, and lively local beach energy.
4:15–6:30 PM
Airbnb pool/reset. Shower, unpack, and ease into the trip.
7:30 PM
Dinner at The Greenhouse. Confirmed reservation.

Friday note

Coki works best as a short first-day food-and-beach stop, not a full lounge day. Leave before it feels too busy and protect the pool reset.

Saturday · June 20

Hook’d brunch + East End beaches + Cuvée

Pretty beach day + nice dinner
9:15–9:30 AM
Leave Charlotte Amalie area. Drive east toward Red Hook.
10:00 AM
Brunch at Hook’d. Waterfront brunch in American Yacht Harbor.
12:00–2:30 PM
Lindqvist / Smith Bay Park. Main beach of the day: pretty, turquoise, calmer-feeling.
3:00–4:30 PM
Optional Sapphire Beach. Late-afternoon drink/second beach if you still want more.
5:00–7:30 PM
Airbnb reset. Pool, shower, get ready without rushing.
8:30 PM
Dinner at Cuvée. Confirmed reservation; nice but not giant-resort-fancy.

Saturday note

Because Cuvée is the dinner anchor, do not overdo the beach hopping. Lindqvist is the priority; Sapphire is optional.

St. Thomas Social brunch + Magens Bay + Caribbean Fish Market

Brunch scene + one classic beach
10:30 AM–12:30/1:00 PM
Brunch at St. Thomas Social. Make this the fun brunch moment, not just a quick breakfast.
1:30–4:00 PM
Magens Bay. One classic St. Thomas beach after brunch: swim, float, relax, and keep it easy.

This is not a beach-hop day. St. Thomas Social plus Magens gives you brunch energy and beach time while still leaving room before dinner.

Monday · June 22

Full St. John day + dinner open

Big beach day, no dinner pressure
Early AM
Drive to Red Hook car ferry. Aim for a morning ferry and build in waiting time.
Morning
Maho Bay. Calm water, turtle/snorkel energy, easy first St. John stop.
Midday
Lunch + second beach. Choose Trunk Bay for iconic scenery or Cinnamon Bay for more space.
By ~4:30 PM
Return toward Cruz Bay car ferry. Do not cut it close; the last return is the constraint.
Evening
Dinner open/flexible. Keep it very low-stakes: Red Hook casual, takeout, pizza, snacks, or Airbnb pool dinner.

Car ferry rule

Since the last car ferry back from St. John is early evening, dinner should stay flexible on St. Thomas. No reservation needed here.

Calm beach + view moment
Morning

Lunch / early afternoon
Airbnb reset. Shower, snack, and pack a little before the tram.
2:00–4:00 PM
Skyride to Paradise Point. Tram/view moment. Re-check the posted schedule the week of travel.
Evening
Dinner at The Longboard St. Thomas. Cute, casual, broad-menu final-night option.

Tuesday note

Secret Harbour gives you a beach every day without making Tuesday feel packed. Keep it to a morning swim/snorkel, then reset before Paradise Point.
