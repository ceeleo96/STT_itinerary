Brewers Bay kills the check-in gap without burning one of the main beach days. Keep swimsuits and sunscreen accessible in a tote.

Monday · June 22

Full St. John day + dinner open

Adventure day, no dinner pressure
Early AM
Drive to Red Hook car ferry. Aim for a morning ferry and build in waiting time.
Morning
Maho Bay. Calm water, turtle/snorkel energy, easy first St. John stop.
Midday
Lunch + second beach. Choose Trunk Bay for iconic scenery or Cinnamon Bay for more space.
By ~4:30 PM
Return toward Cruz Bay car ferry. Do not cut it close; the last return is the constraint.
Evening
Dinner open / flexible. Keep it low-stakes after the ferry day: Red Hook casual, takeout, Airbnb snacks, or decide based on energy.

Car ferry rule

Since the last car ferry back from St. John is early evening, dinner should be on St. Thomas, not St. John — and it should stay easy.
